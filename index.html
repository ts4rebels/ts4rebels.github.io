Instructions

Step 0: Go to the Vault (link above).

Step 1: Click on the Select account button.

Step 2: Click on the Get auth button.

Step 3: A new tab will open. Login with your Google account—pick an account that isn't important to you or create a new one.

Step 4: rclone needs access to your account. Click Allow.

Step 5: Now the page will say "This site can't be reached". Don't panic, this is normal. Just copy the URL on the address bar of this page.

Step 6: Go back to the previous tab and paste the copied URL in the field that says "Paste the redirected link here". Then click Continue.

Step 7: You're logged in! To download files, open any folder, select the files you want, and click Copy. The files will now be in your Google Drive. You can either download them to your computer from this very same window (where it says "My Drive"), or you can do it from your drive.

That is all. Was it that difficult? No, it wasn't; you'll get used to it.
